Do's and Don'ts for responsible social media use as a college student:-

Do's:
 1.Use polite and respectful language while interacting online
 2.Keep your profile professional and appropriate
 3.Verify information before sharing or reposting
 4.Use strong privacy settings to protect your account
 5.Think before posting anything publicly
Don’ts
 1.Do not share personal or sensitive information (phone, OTP, address)
 2.Avoid posting offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content
 3.Do not click on suspicious links or unknown messages
 4.Avoid spreading fake news or unverified information
 5.Do not engage in online arguments or negative discussions
